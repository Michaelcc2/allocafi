# AllocaFi User Education And How-To Messaging

## Beginner Explanation

AllocaFi helps you organize money connected to your wallet. Instead of seeing one balance and guessing what it is for, you can create Virtual Budget Accounts for different spending categories.

## How A New User Starts

1. Open AllocaFi.
2. Add or connect an owner wallet.
3. Choose Free or AllocaFi Core.
4. Review the wallet verification message.
5. Sign a safe ownership verification message.
6. Choose or customize a Budget Template.
7. Review Virtual Budget Accounts.
8. Use the dashboard to monitor cash flow, allocations, bills, reports, and asset records.

## How To Explain Virtual Budget Accounts

Virtual Budget Accounts are not new wallets. They are organizational categories that help users decide what their wallet money is for.

Examples:

- Bills
- Food
- Gas
- Savings
- Emergency
- Vacation
- Business expenses
- Taxes

## How To Explain Virtual Asset Accounts

Virtual Asset Accounts are not custody accounts. They are organizational containers for reserve assets already connected through wallet balances and records.

Examples:

- Bitcoin Reserve
- Solana Reserve
- Ethereum Reserve
- Long-Term Holdings
- Trading Reserve

## How To Explain Legal Core

Legal Core helps organize blockchain-backed asset records such as acquisitions, transfers, cost basis status, classifications, and export-ready records.

Safe wording:
- "Record organization"
- "Classification support"
- "Cost basis status"
- "Export preparation"

Avoid:
- "Legal advice"
- "Guaranteed proof"
- "Guaranteed tax outcome"

## How To Explain Tax Ledger Core

Tax Ledger Core helps prepare reporting records, categories, gain/loss records, and accountant-ready exports.

Safe wording:
- "Tax record preparation"
- "Suggested categories"
- "Accountant-ready exports"
- "Review before filing"

Avoid:
- "Guaranteed deduction"
- "Guaranteed tax savings"
- "Official tax filing advice"

## How To Explain Vault

AllocaFi Vault protects user-owned AllocaFi data, settings, reports, and export history. Sensitive exports require wallet ownership verification.

Key user point:
- "A wallet address alone should never unlock private financial records."
