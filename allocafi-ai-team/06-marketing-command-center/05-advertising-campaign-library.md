# AllocaFi Advertising Campaign Library

## Campaign: Stop Budgeting From One Big Wallet Balance

Hook:
- "Your wallet balance is not a budget."

Problem:
- Users cannot see what money is for.

AllocaFi Angle:
- Virtual Budget Accounts turn wallet money into clear categories.

CTA:
- "Build your stablecoin budget."

## Campaign: Keep Your Keys, Get Organized

Hook:
- "Budget crypto without handing over your keys."

Problem:
- Users want organization but do not want custody risk.

AllocaFi Angle:
- Non-custodial budgeting and record organization.

CTA:
- "Organize your wallet money."

## Campaign: Crypto Records Before Tax Season

Hook:
- "Do not wait until tax season to organize crypto records."

Problem:
- Asset activity becomes difficult to classify later.

AllocaFi Angle:
- Legal Core and Tax Ledger Core organize acquisition, transfer, disposal, gain/loss, and export records.

CTA:
- "Start your crypto record system."

## Campaign: Budget Templates That Feel Built For Real Life

Hook:
- "Pick a budget plan that matches how you actually earn and spend."

Problem:
- Generic budgets do not fit truckers, freelancers, families, creators, or businesses.

AllocaFi Angle:
- Template Library with business, family, crypto, lifestyle, fun, and custom plans.

CTA:
- "Choose your budget template."

## Campaign: A Command Center For Stablecoin Money

Hook:
- "Stablecoin money deserves more than a balance screen."

Problem:
- Users need spending categories, reports, liquidity monitoring, records, and secure exports.

AllocaFi Angle:
- AllocaFi Core as a practical financial operating system.

CTA:
- "Open your AllocaFi command center."
