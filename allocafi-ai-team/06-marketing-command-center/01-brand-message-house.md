# AllocaFi Brand Message House

## Core Positioning

AllocaFi is the operating system for non-custodial stablecoin budgeting, wallet-based financial organization, and blockchain-backed asset records.

## Short Description

AllocaFi helps users organize wallet money into Virtual Budget Accounts, track crypto reserves through Virtual Asset Accounts, protect financial records with Vault, and prepare cleaner Legal Core and Tax Ledger records without giving up custody of their funds.

## Simple One-Liner

AllocaFi helps you organize crypto money like a real financial system without handing over your keys.

## Tagline Options

- The operating system for stablecoin budgeting.
- Organize spending. Track wealth. Keep custody.
- Budget your wallet money without giving up control.
- A financial command center for non-custodial crypto users.
- Wallet-powered budgeting, records, and recovery.

## Audience Promise

AllocaFi gives users a structured way to manage wallet-based money, spending categories, asset records, reports, and exports while keeping control of their own wallets.

## What AllocaFi Is

- A non-custodial financial organization platform.
- A stablecoin budgeting interface.
- A Virtual Budget Account system.
- A Virtual Asset Account system.
- A Legal Core and Tax Ledger record layer.
- A Vault-backed recovery and export system.

## What AllocaFi Is Not

- Not a bank.
- Not a custodial exchange.
- Not a crypto wallet replacement.
- Not a private key recovery service.
- Not tax, legal, or investment advice.

## Message Pillars

### Organize Spending

Virtual Budget Accounts let users separate wallet funds into categories like Bills, Food, Gas, Savings, Emergency, Business, and custom accounts.

### Track Wealth

Virtual Asset Accounts help users organize BTC, ETH, SOL, and other reserve assets by purpose, account type, and Legal Core record status.

### Preserve Control

AllocaFi does not custody crypto. Users keep control through their own wallets.

### Improve Records

Legal Core and Tax Ledger Core help create cleaner asset, gain/loss, export, and report records.

### Recover Experience

Vault helps protect and restore user-owned AllocaFi data and export packages through verified wallet ownership.
