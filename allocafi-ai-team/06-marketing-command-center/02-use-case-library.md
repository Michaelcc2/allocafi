# AllocaFi Use Case Library

## Personal Stablecoin Budgeting

User:
- Everyday crypto user receiving stablecoins or holding wallet balances.

Problem:
- Wallet balances show one pile of money, not a real budget.

AllocaFi Use:
- User creates Virtual Budget Accounts for Bills, Food, Gas, Savings, Emergency, and custom goals.
- User sees money organized without moving funds into custody.

Market Message:
- "Turn one wallet balance into a clear personal budget."

## Trucking And Owner Operator Budgeting

User:
- Truck driver, owner operator, or vehicle-based contractor.

Problem:
- Fuel, maintenance, insurance, truck payment, taxes, and emergency reserves are hard to separate.

AllocaFi Use:
- User applies an Owner Operator budget template.
- Funds are organized into business-specific Virtual Budget Accounts.

Market Message:
- "Built for real cash flow: fuel, maintenance, taxes, and income reserve."

## Freelancer And Creator Income

User:
- Freelancer, creator, consultant, or self-employed worker.

Problem:
- Income arrives unevenly and tax reserves are easy to forget.

AllocaFi Use:
- User applies Freelancer or Creator Mode templates.
- User tracks income reserve, business expenses, software, taxes, savings, and personal pay.

Market Message:
- "Smooth irregular income before it turns into stress."

## Family Budgeting

User:
- Household or family managing shared goals.

Problem:
- Family spending, bills, savings, and emergency money are scattered.

AllocaFi Use:
- User applies Family Essentials, Single Parent Plan, or Family Legacy templates.
- Family dashboards can organize shared goals and future household planning.

Market Message:
- "Give the household budget a shared command center."

## Crypto Reserve Tracking

User:
- BTC, ETH, SOL, or long-term crypto holder.

Problem:
- Wallets show balances but not financial purpose, cost basis status, or records.

AllocaFi Use:
- User opens Virtual Asset Accounts like Bitcoin Reserve or Solana Reserve.
- Legal Core tracks acquisition, price basis status, gain/loss estimate, and record readiness.

Market Message:
- "Organize crypto reserves like wealth accounts, not random wallet balances."

## Legal Core Records

User:
- Crypto user who wants cleaner blockchain-backed asset records.

Problem:
- It is hard to know what was acquired, transferred, disposed, or still needs classification.

AllocaFi Use:
- Legal Core organizes acquisition records, transfer history, disposal classification, cost basis status, and export readiness.

Market Message:
- "Keep cleaner records for the crypto you already control."

## Tax Ledger Preparation

User:
- User or business preparing transaction categories and gain/loss records.

Problem:
- Tax season is messy when wallet data, receipts, categories, and reports are scattered.

AllocaFi Use:
- Tax Ledger Core organizes category suggestions, gain/loss records, reports, and accountant-ready exports.

Market Message:
- "Prepare better records before tax season becomes a scramble."

## Vault And Export Recovery

User:
- User who wants their AllocaFi setup, reports, and records protected across sessions and devices.

Problem:
- Financial setup gets lost when switching devices or rebuilding an app profile.

AllocaFi Use:
- Vault protects user-owned AllocaFi data and requires verified wallet ownership for sensitive exports.

Market Message:
- "Your dashboard, records, and exports should come back when you do."
