# AllocaFi Problem And Solution Library

## Problem: Wallets Show Balances, Not Budgets

Customer Pain:
- Users see money but not purpose.
- Bills, food, savings, and emergency money all look mixed together.

AllocaFi Solution:
- Virtual Budget Accounts organize wallet balances into spending categories.

Subscriber Value:
- Better clarity, less confusion, faster decisions.

## Problem: Crypto Users Need Records

Customer Pain:
- Asset purchases, transfers, and disposals are hard to track.
- Cost basis can be missing or wrong.

AllocaFi Solution:
- Legal Core and Tax Ledger Core organize asset records, price basis status, classifications, and exports.

Subscriber Value:
- Cleaner records and better preparation for review, reports, and accountants.

## Problem: New Users Do Not Understand Stablecoin Budgeting

Customer Pain:
- Crypto budgeting feels technical.
- Users do not know where to start.

AllocaFi Solution:
- Budget Template Library gives professional starting plans like Essential Budget, Owner Operator, Freelancer, Family Essentials, Stablecoin Income, and more.

Subscriber Value:
- Faster onboarding and easier first success.

## Problem: Financial Apps Ask Users To Give Up Control

Customer Pain:
- Users worry about custody, frozen accounts, or wallet access risk.

AllocaFi Solution:
- AllocaFi remains non-custodial and does not store private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases.

Subscriber Value:
- Organization without surrendering wallet control.

## Problem: Records And Reports Get Scattered

Customer Pain:
- Receipts, reports, exports, categories, and dashboard preferences are fragmented.

AllocaFi Solution:
- Vault protects user-owned AllocaFi data and supports secure export workflows.

Subscriber Value:
- Better continuity across sessions, devices, and reporting needs.

## Problem: Business Owners Need Practical Cash Flow Categories

Customer Pain:
- Business income must cover operations, payroll, taxes, marketing, profit, and emergency reserves.

AllocaFi Solution:
- Business templates organize accounts for owner operators, small businesses, freelancers, side hustles, and creators.

Subscriber Value:
- A practical financial operating structure from day one.
